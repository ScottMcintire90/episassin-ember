OUR GREAT AND NOBLE HERO, SARUMAN THE WHITE, ALSO KNOWN AS SARUMAN OF MANY COLORS.

Saruman, the man who brought us the great Uruk-Hai. While it has been said by the detractors of Sauron, our great Lord and Savior that he is nothing but a pawn whose hunger for power led directly to the takeover of the Shire even after the death of our Lord and Savior and the enslavement of all those adowable wittle Hobbits.

This could not be further from the truth! Saruman is a great and noble hero to our cause.
